Goals of Frene Education

The goal of Frene education, which started based on Jean-Celestin Frene education in France in the 1920s, is to develop the ability of children to analyze situations on their own no matter what problems they face, respond to them according to their abilities, and take full responsibility for their own work. is to grow it.

Finnish education in Northern Europe, which ranked high in the evaluation of the Program for International Student Assessment (PISA), actively utilizes the Frene education method.

Training Program of Frene

Babies have an amazing ability to pattern whole objects and learn them all at once. At Frene, classes are conducted with appropriate and systematic textbooks according to the child's developmental process.

Babies who are born with huge learning abilities have a genius of infinite capacity as they approach 0 years of age. But as time goes on, that amazing genius also diminishes and becomes fixed.

Therefore, it can be seen that the most important thing in early childhood education is not education until 3-6 years old, but education conducted by mother at home from immediately after birth. Psychologists also believe that a child's intellectual curiosity takes root in the first few months after birth.

Features of Frene Education

Right-Brain Play Education
What infants and toddlers experience through play is connected to brain wiring and learning. Right-brain learning through fun play makes children rich in creativity and emotion, the core elements of the 4th industrial revolution.
Customized Education
Adjust the difficulty level to suit each child's different inclinations, environments, and characteristics. It is an education that brings out the strengths, potentials, and self-esteem of the child.
Convergence (Integration) Education
It does not stop at simply acquiring knowledge. Expand knowledge through various textbooks, satisfy curiosity, and raise children to think. Your child will learn insight based on a wealth of background knowledge about things.
Character Education
Sufficient interaction with a positive-minded teacher helps establish good character and desirable habits.
Future-Oriented Education
The talents required in the era of the 4th revolution are those who lead life. It stimulates curiosity, which is the basis of learning, and allows you to express your feelings and thoughts freely and in various ways.
Teacher Differentiation
We have the best teachers with the belief that 'the quality of education cannot exceed the quality of teachers'. Through continuous teacher education, we study to effectively and effectively lead the play education, which is much more difficult and difficult than on-the-ground learning.
